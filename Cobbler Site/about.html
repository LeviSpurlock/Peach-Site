About

The Peach Cobbler Factory was started by a little couple who came to Nashville from New Orleans after the disaster of Hurricane Katrina. Tammy and Juan Edgerton began selling their delicious cobblers at the Farmers Market in 2013 before they finally decided to create the home base in at Woodbine.

They don't plan to stop there! The Edgerton's have big plans to expand the menu past just cobblers to create the full dining experience! Soon enough you will be able to experience everything the Peach Cobbler Factory has to offer.
